About me:

As you may have noticed from above. I'm really a bit more Clark Kent than superman.
I have had a lifelong love for technology. I have been around it as long as I can remember, and it has progressively become a bigger part of my life as I've grown.

The thing that really nudged me over the edge from being a bystander marveling at technology to embarking on a path to master it was a website. It was actually not a good website, either. I was working for a small company at the time, and our company (as well as industry as a whole) vastly underutilized technology. I began working more on the digital footprint of our company and realized very quickly that it was something challenging and something powerful that I could not just make a career out of, but make a lifelong pursuit of honing skills.

The further I delved into bootcamps, going back to school, or teaching myself, I very quickly realized that I needed a quick way to transition myself, and get into the tech field where I could continue lifelong learning and problem solving.

Here I am now, I have a small toolkit, but it is very rapidly expanding!

If you would like to know more, please shoot me a message via any of my channels in my contact me section, or check out my blog. I would love to hear from you, and learn about your journey!
