About me:

I have had a lifelong love for technology. I have been around it as long as I can remember, and it has progressively become a bigger part of my life as I've grown.

The thing that really nudged me over the edge from being a bystander, marveling at technology, to embarking on a path to master it was a website. It was actually not a good website, either. I was working for a small company at the time, and our company (as well as industry as a whole) vastly underutilized technology. I began working more on the digital footprint of our company and realized very quickly that it was something challenging and something powerful that I could not just make a career out of, but make a lifelong passion pursuit.

The further I delved into bootcamps, going back to school, or teaching myself, I realized that I wanted a quick way to transition myself. I wanted to dive head-first into the tech field where I could continue lifelong learning and problem solving.

If you would like to know more, please shoot me a message via any of my channels in my contact me section, or check out my blog. I would love to get to know you, and any opportunity that you may have for me to grow and help your team knock down challenges!
